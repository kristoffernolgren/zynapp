We experience life, we make footprints and we collect memories. We believe that finding, viewing and organizing your digital information can be done in a smarter way, more like we humans naturally think.

Collecting footprints from your apps

 

  • The list

    All footprints gathered in a list as they come in, already automatically tagged. Surf the tags, explore and organize your digital life the way you want it! Zynapp will learn your personal preferences and sort things where they belong.

  • People

    As contact-app it’s both fun and gives you smart sorting features. But the true power comes when you use it as a starting point for finding information​

  • People list

    Truly-everyday-useful! For business or pleasure, this shows all the information related to one of your contacts, such as photos, e-mails, events, places, files and posts in social media feeds displayed in the list view!

  • The timeline

    For a new perspective, use the Timeline view where you can filter all your content related to a specific time and then switch to other tabs to see for example all the photos collected from all of your devices related to an event.

  • Places

    Zynapp gives you the unique possiblity to get a geographical view on all your content, which is a very useful way to find related information by zooming in on a place.

How does it work? 

 Zynapp automatically downloads the content from your favorite apps and services and tags every little piece of data in the background. 

 

Your information in one place

The first thing you will notice when you start using Zynapp is that you are now able to view all your content together in one place, in one view. Either in a timeline, on a map, in a list or in the special contact view.

Find your content in a smart way

The second thing you will start using is filtering. Now, that’s quite a cool way of finding what you are looking for, because you can filter all your content by any type or tag while you switch between the different views. Just unmark a filter and you return to see it all!
